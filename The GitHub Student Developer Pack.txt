The GitHub Student Developer Pack provides a wide range of free or discounted tools, services, and resources for verified students (typically requiring school email or student ID verification through GitHub). These offers are available while you maintain student status, with some having specific durations like 1 year or 6 months. Below is a categorized breakdown of the current offers as of November 2025, including what each provides.

### Developer Tools
- **GitHub Pro**: Free access to advanced features like private repositories and collaboration tools.
- **GitHub Copilot**: Free Pro access for AI-powered code suggestions and completions.
- **GitHub Codespaces**: Free Pro-level cloud-based development environments.
- **GitHub Pages**: Free static website hosting (one site per account/organization, unlimited project sites).
- **JetBrains**: Free annual subscription to IDEs like IntelliJ, PyCharm, etc.
- **Visual Studio Code**: Free coding packs for Java, Python, or .NET setups.
- **Testmail**: Free Essential plan for email testing.
- **Termius**: Free Pro/Team features for SSH and terminal access (including mobile).
- **GitLens**: Free access, plus integration with GitKraken (free for 6 months, then up to 80% off Pro).
- **GitKraken**: Student Plan free for 6 months, then up to 80% off Pro.
- **Tower**: Free Pro license for Git client.
- **WorkingCopy**: All Pro features free (mobile Git client).
- **GitHub Desktop**: Free open-source desktop Git client.
- **Xojo**: Free Pro license for cross-platform app development.
- **LocalStack**: Free license for AWS emulator in cloud environments.
- **Appwrite**: Free Education plan (equivalent to Pro, worth $15/month) with high limits.
- **Requestly**: Free Professional plan (worth $270) for 1 year (API mocking/debugging).
- **PopSQL**: Free Premium subscription for collaborative SQL editing.
- **SQLGate**: Access to most Standard features for 1 year.
- **ToDiagram**: Free Pro Plan with full editor, no data limits, up to 10 cloud documents (no AI).
- **Deepnote**: Free Team plan with unlimited projects, 30-day history, premium integrations, and up to 5GB RAM machines.
- **Doppler**: Free Team subscription for secret management.
- **Imgbot**: Free image optimization for all repositories.
